Together We Can Change

Winston Churchill once said, “I am always ready to learn although I do not always like being taught.” In fact, it will be even harder if the person is a teacher himself/herself. But Galileo Galilei had said “You cannot teach a man anything. You can only help him discover it within himself.” As a resource person to various Faculty development program, I am practicing this approach.

We know sharing is caring. I share with the participants many professional practices relevant to technical education. I also motivate the faculty to adopt competitive teaching and learning methods, so that they can outperform their peers.


I train the faculty members to systematically improve their skills in the following areas :

  • Online educational tools
  • Integration of ICT for teaching learning activities
  • Instructional design principles
  • Different methods to engage the students
  • Personal development
  • Skills in designing, developing, and implementing best practices
  • Development of massive open online courses
  • Open educational resources

As a UGC-HRDC resource person. I have been regularly contributing to various Faculty development program (FDP) and Faculty Induction program (FIP). The different subject areas include :

  • Green chemistry
  • Bioorthogonal chemistry
  • Massive open online courses (MOOC)
  • Application of tools and techniques to create, edit and evaluate online lectures.
